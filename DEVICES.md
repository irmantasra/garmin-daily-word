# Supported devices

The app targets **64 glance-capable Garmin watches** (auto-generated from installed SDK device definitions).

- **48 devices** get the glance widget + full app.
- **16 older devices** (API < 3.5) get the full app only — the glance is unsupported and gracefully skipped; open the app from the app list.
